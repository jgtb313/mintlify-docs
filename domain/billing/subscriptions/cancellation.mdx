---
title: "Subscription Cancellation"
description: "Cancellation flow with partial failure handling."
---

## Flow

1. Cancel Stripe plan subscription
2. Cancel Stripe fee subscription (if exists)
3. Status transitions to `CANCELLED`

## Partial Failures

If a Stripe cancellation fails, the subscription transitions to `CANCELLATION_PENDING` and the failure is recorded in a dedicated CancellationIssue table.

## Effects

- Organization `subscriptionId` set to `null`
- New costs are no longer recorded
- Active SubscriptionCoupons transition to `ENDED`
