title: "Billing Thresholds"
description: "Spending thresholds for organizations."
---

## Overview

Billing thresholds define spending limits per billing cycle. Each organization is assigned one threshold matching its currency.

## Usage

- Displayed in upcoming invoice previews
- Snapshot captured at invoice creation (InvoiceBillingThreshold)
- Used for monitoring and alerting on spending

## Assignment

Thresholds are assigned at organization creation and can be updated by admins. The threshold must match the organization's currency.
