## Coupon Discounts

Coupons are applied to invoices based on their duration:
- **ONCE**: Applied to a single invoice
- **REPEATING**: Applied to each invoice for `durationInMonths` billing cycles

Discount calculation:
- **FIXED**: Absolute amount deducted (in cents)
- **PERCENTAGE**: Percentage of invoice total

Each application creates a `CouponUsage` record.

## Voucher Discounts

Vouchers (billing credits) are automatically applied to invoices by Stripe:
- Credits deducted from the voucher balance
- Each application creates a `VoucherUsage` record
- Optional fee restrictions limit credits to specific fee invoices

## Combined Discounts

Invoices can have both coupon and voucher discounts. The total `discountAmount` is the sum of all applied discounts.
