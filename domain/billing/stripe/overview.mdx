## Integration Model

AWSales uses Stripe as the payment processor. Data flows bidirectionally:

**AWSales → Stripe**:
- Products (Plans, Fees)
- Prices (PlanIntervals)
- Customers (Organizations)
- Subscriptions
- Coupons + Promotion Codes
- Credit Grants (Vouchers)
- Billing Meters (usage reporting)

**Stripe → AWSales**:
- Invoice status updates (paid, failed, refunded)
- Payment method events
- Dispute notifications
