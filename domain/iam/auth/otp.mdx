## Contexts

| Context | Purpose | Next Step |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| `EMAIL_VERIFICATION` | Verify email after sign-up (also used for resend) | [Email Verification](/studio-api/auth/email-verification) |
| `MAGIC_AUTH` | Passwordless login | [Sign In Magic Auth](/studio-api/auth/sign-in-magic-auth) |
| `RESET_PASSWORD` | Reset password | [Reset Password](/studio-api/auth/reset-password) |

## Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send OTP">
    Call [Send OTP](/studio-api/otp/send-otp) with `recipient` and `context`. Returns `otpId`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate OTP">
    Call [Validate OTP](/studio-api/otp/verify-otp) with `otpId` in path, plus `recipient`, `context`, and `code`. Returns 204.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Action">
    Call the context-specific endpoint with `otpId` and `code` to complete the flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Recipient

The `recipient` field accepts either an email address or phone number.
