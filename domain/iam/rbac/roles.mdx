## Role Structure

Each role has:
- **slug**: Unique identifier within its scope
- **name**: Human-readable name
- **type**: `ENVIRONMENT_ROLE` or `ORGANIZATION_ROLE`

## Environment Roles

Platform-wide roles defined at the system level. Cannot be modified or deleted at the organization level. All organizations inherit these roles.

## Organization Roles

Custom roles scoped to a specific organization. Created, updated, and deleted by organization admins.

## Role Assignment

Roles are assigned to users through [Memberships](/domain/iam/multi-tenancy/memberships). Each membership assigns exactly one role to a user within an organization.
