---
title: "Delete Payment Method"
api: "DELETE /organizations/{organizationId}/payment-methods/{paymentMethodId}"
description: "Delete a payment method."
---

Deletes a payment method by its ID.

<Info>Requires `payment_method:delete` permission.</Info>

## Headers

<ParamField header="Authorization" type="string" required>
  Bearer token.
</ParamField>

## Path Parameters

<ParamField path="organizationId" type="string" required>
  The organization UUID.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="paymentMethodId" type="string" required>
  The payment method ID.
</ParamField>
