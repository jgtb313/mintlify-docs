## Conventions

- **IDs**: UUIDv7 — time-ordered UUIDs that enable cursor-based pagination
- **Monetary amounts**: Stored as integers in **cents** (e.g., `4900` = $49.00)
- **Currencies**: `USD`, `BRL`, `EUR`
- **Timestamps**: ISO 8601, stored as UTC
- **Soft deletes**: `deletedAt` / `deletedBy` fields where applicable
- **Audit trail**: `createdBy`, `updatedBy`, `deletedBy` for major entities
- **External refs**: `externalRef` fields store identifiers from external providers, linking internal entities to their counterparts in third-party systems
