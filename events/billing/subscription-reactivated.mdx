---
title: "subscription.reactivated"
description: "Published when a past due subscription is reactivated after payment recovery."
---

## Event Details

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Type** | `subscription.reactivated` |

## Payload Schema

<ResponseField name="subscriptionId" type="string (UUID v7)" required>

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="pausedBy" type="string (UUID)">

<ResponseField name="cancelledBy" type="string (UUID)">

  User who last updated the subscription.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="updatedAt" type="string (ISO 8601)" required>
  Last update timestamp.
</ResponseField>
