---
title: "The Payment Method Object"
---

The payment method object is a discriminated union based on the `type` field. The shape varies depending on whether the type is `CARD` or `BOLETO`.

## Card

<ResponseField name="paymentMethodId" type="string">
  The payment method unique identifier.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="organizationId" type="string">
  The organization ID.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="type" type="string">
  The payment method type. Literal `CARD`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="brand" type="string">
  The card brand (e.g. `visa`, `mastercard`).
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="last4" type="string">
  The last four digits of the card number.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="expMonth" type="integer">
  The card expiration month.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="expYear" type="integer">
  The card expiration year.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="isDefault" type="boolean">
  Whether this is the default payment method.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="billingDetails" type="object">
  The billing details associated with this payment method.
  <Expandable title="billing details properties">
    <ResponseField name="name" type="string">
      The cardholder name.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="email" type="string">
      The billing email address.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="phone" type="string">
      The billing phone number.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="address" type="object">
      The billing address.
    </ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

</ResponseExample>

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="organizationId" type="string">

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="type" type="string">
  The payment method type. Literal `BOLETO`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="isDefault" type="boolean">

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="billingDetails" type="object">
  The billing details associated with this payment method.
  <Expandable title="billing details properties">
